ParkSafe Brisbane

DECO7180 · Design Computing Studio 2 · University of Queensland

Developer: Dai Pan

Purpose

ParkSafe is a vehicle-theft awareness prototype for Brisbane. It combines Queensland Police Service open data with a map-led route view so drivers can see relative risk along a journey—not as a legal or insurance verdict, but as practical context before they park.

Approach

The interface uses Leaflet and OpenStreetMap, static risk zones derived from the course dataset, and an optional AI summary via a small Flask backend (Gemini). The studio build prioritises clarity and iteration over production-scale data pipelines.

Privacy

There is no login and no personal profile stored in the app. Route inputs stay in your browser unless you request an AI summary, in which case the composed prompt is sent to our server and then to Google’s API—subject to their terms. Do not paste sensitive personal data into the tool.
